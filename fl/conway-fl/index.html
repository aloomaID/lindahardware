Does pea gravel attract pests in Conway Florida ?

Pea gravel does not attract pests and is a great choice for pest-free landscaping . It also allows water to drain properly, reducing standing water that could attract mosquitoes.

How long does pea gravel last in landscaping

Pea gravel is highly durable and can last for many years with minimal maintenance, making it an excellent long-term investment for landscaping projects in Conway Florida .

Is pea gravel a good option for erosion control

Yes, pea gravel helps with erosion control by promoting water drainage and preventing soil erosion on slopes and hills .
