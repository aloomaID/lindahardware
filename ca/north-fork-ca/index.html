North Fork, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

North Fork (formerly Brown's and Northfork; Mono wa?ahhpY', 'cedar grove') is a unincorporated community in Madera County, California, United States. As of the 2020 United States census it had a population of 3,250. For statistical purposes, the United States Census Bureau has defined North Fork as a census-designated place (CDP).
